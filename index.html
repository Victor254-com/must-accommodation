Hostel Rules & Regulations:

MERU UNIVERSITY HOSTEL RULES & REGULATIONS 2026

1. Eligibility & Allocation

  • All regular students are eligible to apply for university accommodation.
  • Priority during high demand: New students, First-years, Students with special needs.
  • Allocation is strictly first-come-first-served upon successful payment of booking fee.

2. Booking Procedure

  • Non-refundable booking fee: Ksh 1,500 (deducted from semester accommodation fees).
  • Submit your application via this official portal only.
  • For inquiries: accommodation@must.ac.ke or +254 758 903 000

3. Safety & Conduct

  • Respect all university staff and fellow students.
  • Report lost keys immediately: Replacement fee Ksh 500.
  • No unauthorized guests or visitors overnight.
  • Cooking strictly in designated kitchen areas only.
  • Quiet hours: 10:00PM – 6:00AM daily.
  • University not liable for personal property loss or damage.

4. Prohibited Items

  • Unauthorized electrical appliances, heaters, cooking devices.
  • Alcohol, illicit drugs, weapons, and flammable materials.
  • Unapproved extension cables or power boards.

5. Damages & Vacating

  • All damages will be charged to the responsible student.
  • Rooms must be fully vacated by end of every semester.
  • Check-in and check-out inspections are mandatory.
  • University reserves right to re-allocate or terminate accommodation for rule breach.

Important Official Notice:

OFFICIAL NOTICE TO ADMITTED STUDENTS

Following the release of 2026 KUCCPS placement results, the Meru University of Science and Technology Accommodation Department wishes to inform all first-year students that hostel spaces are limited and will be allocated on a first-come-first-served basis.


To secure your accommodation, you are required to pay a mandatory booking fee of KES 1,500 via the official payment channel provided. This amount will be deducted from your total semester accommodation fees upon admission.


Students are advised to complete this process early to avoid missing out on hostel space. The balance of your accommodation fees must be settled within the first week of reporting to the university.


Official Procedure:

  1. Complete payment as instructed on the next page.
  2. Submit payment verification details correctly.
  3. Receive confirmation email within 12 working hours.
  4. Print and present your booking confirmation slip during admission.

⚠️ DO NOT make payments to any other number or account not listed here.
