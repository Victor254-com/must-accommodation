Key Rules & Regulations:

MERU UNIVERSITY HOSTEL RULES & REGULATIONS 2026

1. Eligibility & Allocation

  • All students can apply for university accommodation.
  • Priority during high demand goes to: New students, First-years, Students with physical challenges.
  • Allocation is done on first-come-first-served basis after payment of Ksh 1,500.

2. Booking Procedure

  • You must pay the required booking fee of Ksh 1,500 to secure your bed.
  • Fill out the University Accommodation Form during online registration.
  • Continuing students may be required to email details to: accommodation@mu.ac.ke
  • The Ksh 1,500 booking fee is NON-REFUNDABLE and forms part of semester hostel fees.

3. Safety & Conduct

  • Students must conduct themselves with maturity and respect at all times.
  • Misplaced keys must be reported immediately. Replacement cost Ksh 500.
  • Sharing rooms with unauthorized guests is strictly prohibited.
  • Cooking in rooms is NOT allowed outside of designated kitchen areas.
  • Excessive noise during quiet hours 10:00PM - 6:00AM is prohibited.
  • The university is not liable for loss of personal property.

4. Prohibited Items

  • Appliances like hot plates, electric coils, and unauthorized high-wattage heaters are banned.
  • Alcohol, drugs, weapons, and flammable substances are strictly prohibited.
  • Extension boxes must be approved by hostel management.

5. Damages & Vacating

  • Any damage to university property will be charged to the occupant.
  • Rooms must be vacated at the end of every semester/academic year.
  • Students must participate in check-in and check-out inspections.
  • The university reserves the right to re-allocate or evict students for breach of rules.

Important Notice:

NOTICE TO FIRST-YEAR STUDENTS

Due to the high influx of KUCCPS-placed students, the Meru University of Science and Technology (MUST) Accommodation Office, under Madam Maureen Kyalo, is prioritizing hostel reservations for students who book in advance.


If you are interested in residing in the university hostels, you must secure your room by paying a booking fee of KES 1,500 before all spaces are occupied. This booking fee will be deducted from your overall accommodation fee for the semester.


To help students facing financial difficulties, the department now allows you to pay this booking fee first, while clearing the rest of the semester's accommodation fee within the first week after admission.


Steps to secure your room:

  • 1. Make your payment through the instructions on the next page.
  • 2. You will receive an email within 12 hours containing your room booking ticket.
  • 3. Print and submit this ticket for confirmation on your day of admission.

Book your room early to avoid missing out. Stay safe!
